Yantra (Surgical Blunt Instruments Compendium)

Yantra Definition

तत्र, मनःशरीराबाधकराणि शल्यानि; तेषामाहरणोपायो यन्त्राणि ||४||

Any structural apparatus utilized to extract external or extraneous foreign elements (Shalya) causing trauma to the body or mind is called a Yantra.

Yantra Types (Sankhya System)

Acharya Sushruth (101 Total)

  1. Swastik Yantra (24)
  2. Sandansh Yantra (2)
  3. Tala Yantra (2)
  4. Nadi Yantra (20)
  5. Shalaka Yantra (28)
  6. Upayantra (25)

Yantra Guna (Qualities)

  1. Samahitani (Equally aligned) 2) Khara mukha (Furrowed) 3) Shlakshna Mukha (Smooth) 4) Surupa 5) Sudrudha 6) Sugraha

Yantra Dosha (Mechanical Flaws)

  • Atisthula (Too thick)
  • Asara (Weak)
  • Ati-dirgha (Too long)
  • Ati-hrasva (Too short)
